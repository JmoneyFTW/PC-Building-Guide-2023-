Ultimate PC Building Guide 2023

By: Jailyn Jones / Aug 29, 2023



Greetings everyone! I'm excited to welcome you to today's blog post, where I'll be sharing my journey and passion for PC building over the past four years. My fascination with constructing PCs began immediately after high school graduation, as I utilized the money I received to assemble my very first rig. This initial build prominently featured the unimpressive Ryzen 3 1200 CPU and the GTX 1660 Super graphics card. This endeavor was particularly meaningful to me since I had been a console gamer for as long as I can remember; the shift towards PC building and gaming was unexpected but incredibly fulfilling.

If you've landed on this blog post, it's likely that you share an interest in PC building. The good news is that I've curated a selection of optimal price-to-performance components that are currently available. It's important to note that prices within the realm of PC components tend to fluctuate over time. With that said, let's move beyond the introductions and dive right into the process of constructing our ultimate gaming setup!


Parts needed to complete a full PC Build:


  • CPU (Central Processing Unit)
  • GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)
  • PSU (Power Supply Unit)
  • RAM (Random Access Memory)
  • Storage (HDD or SSD)
  • Computer Chassis (Case)
  • Case (Computer Case)
